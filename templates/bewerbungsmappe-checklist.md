# Bewerbungsmappe Checklist
## Complete Application Portfolio for Renting in Germany

**For:** International employees of PAKA GmbH relocating to Germany
**Purpose:** Assemble this before your first housing viewings

---

## Document Checklist

### Mandatory Documents

- [ ] **Passport copy** (data page + photo page) — color scan, all 4 corners visible
- [ ] **Residence permit copy** (Aufenthaltserlaubnis / EU Blue Card) — front and back
- [ ] **Mieterselbstauskunft** (Tenant Self-Disclosure Form)
  - Download a free template from: https://www.mieterbund.de or ask the landlord for their version
  - Fill in: name, date of birth, profession, income, current housing situation, number of people moving in, pets (yes/no), smoker (yes/no)

### Income Proof (choose what applies)

- [ ] **Last 3 months' payslips** (Gehaltsabrechnungen) — OR —
- [ ] **Employment contract** (Arbeitsvertrag) — if payslips not yet available — AND/OR —
- [ ] **Employer confirmation letter from PAKA GmbH** (see `/templates/employer-confirmation-letter.md`)

### Credit History

- [ ] **SCHUFA report** (Bonitätsauskunft) — order at https://www.meineschufa.de for €29.95
  - Free annual self-disclosure also available (takes ~1 week by post)
  - **If no SCHUFA yet (newcomer):** substitute with bank statements + employer letter + offer to pay 3 months Kaution upfront

### Banking

- [ ] **Last 3 months' bank statements** (Kontoauszüge)
  - German account preferred; foreign bank statements (in English) are also acceptable
  - Redact sensitive transaction details if preferred; keep balance and income visible

### Previous Tenancy (if applicable)

- [ ] **Mietschuldenfreiheitsbescheinigung** (Rent Debt-Free Certificate from previous landlord)
  - Request from your last landlord (in Morocco or elsewhere)
  - Have it officially translated into German or English
  - **If unavailable:** provide a reference letter from previous landlord (Vermieterreferenz) in English

### Optional but Helpful

- [ ] **Cover letter in German** (Anschreiben) — 1 paragraph: who you are, your job, why you are moving to this city, move-in date, how long you plan to stay
- [ ] **Professional photo** — recent, friendly, professional (optional but common in Germany)
- [ ] **Guarantor letter** (Bürgschaftserklärung) — if requested by landlord; PAKA GmbH may provide this

---

## How to Assemble the PDF

1. Scan all documents (use CamScanner, Adobe Scan, or any scanner app)
2. Combine into a **single PDF** in this order:
   1. Cover letter (Anschreiben)
   2. Mieterselbstauskunft (filled out)
   3. Passport + Residence Permit
   4. Payslips / Employment Contract
   5. Employer letter from PAKA GmbH
   6. SCHUFA report (or substitute)
   7. Bank statements
   8. Mietschuldenfreiheitsbescheinigung / Reference letter
3. Name the file: `Bewerbung_[FirstName]_[LastName].pdf`
4. Keep file size below 5 MB (compress if needed at https://ilovepdf.com/compress_pdf)

---

## Before the Viewing (Besichtigung)

- **Bring:** Printed copy of full application portfolio (in a clean folder)
- **Bring:** Original documents for landlord to verify (passport, permit, payslips)
- **Dress:** Neat, professional — first impressions matter in Germany
- **Punctuality:** Arrive exactly on time (Germans take punctuality seriously)
- **Questions to ask:**
  - Ist der Preis warm oder kalt? (Is the price warm or cold rent?)
  - Welche Nebenkosten kommen noch dazu? (What additional utility costs are there?)
  - Ist Internetanschluss inklusive? (Is internet included?)
  - Wie lange läuft der Mietvertrag? (How long is the lease?)
  - Wann könnte ich einziehen? (When could I move in?)
  - Ist eine Untervermietung erlaubt? (Is subletting allowed?)

---

## After Signing the Lease

- **Within 14 days:** Complete Anmeldung (address registration) at Bürgeramt
  - Bring: signed lease or Wohnungsgeberbestätigung from landlord, passport, residence permit
- **Day 1:** Open German bank account for direct debit (SEPA Lastschrift) of rent
- **Month 1:** Set up standing order (Dauerauftrag) for rent payment on agreed date
- **Month 2:** Request SCHUFA report to verify your data is correctly registered
- **Document:** Keep copies of all signed documents and all payment receipts
