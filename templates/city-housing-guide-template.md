## 11. Required Documents Checklist

| Document | German Term | Notes for Non-EU Nationals |
|---|---|---|
| Passport + Residence Permit | Reisepass + Aufenthaltserlaubnis | Min. 1-year permit for WBS |
| Tenant self-declaration | Mieterselbstauskunft | Download free template online |
| Last 3 months' payslips | Gehaltsabrechnungen | Employment contract if no payslips yet |
| Employment contract | Arbeitsvertrag | — |
| SCHUFA report | SCHUFA-BonitätsAuskunft | €29.95 at meineschufa.de |
| Bank statements (3 months) | Kontoauszüge | German or foreign account |
| Rent debt-free certificate | Mietschuldenfreiheitsbescheinigung | From previous landlord; translate if abroad |
| Employer guarantee letter | Bürgschaftserklärung | PAKA GmbH can provide this |

## 12. Tips for Competitive Applications

1. **Write the cover letter in German.** Use DeepL.com.
2. **Respond within hours.** Same-day response expected.
3. **Offer higher deposit.** 3 months Kaltmiete signals stability.
4. **Include a photo.** Optional but common in Germany.
5. **Use PAKA GmbH employer letter.** Substitutes for SCHUFA when missing.
6. **Target private landlords.** More flexible on documentation.
7. **Join WGs first.** Personality matters more than paperwork.

## 13. Common Pitfalls to Avoid

| Pitfall | Risk | Prevention |
|---|---|---|
| Paying deposit before contract | Scam | Never pay without signed Mietvertrag |
| Missing 14-day Anmeldung | Legal issues | Register within 14 days at Bürgeramt |
| Fake subletting | Eviction | Verify landlord's permission |
| Registering fake address | Criminal offense | Only register where you live |
