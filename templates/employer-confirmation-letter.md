# Employer Confirmation Letter Template
## For PAKA GmbH — Supporting Employee Housing Applications in Germany

---

**How to use this template:**
- Fill in all `[BRACKETED]` fields
- Print on PAKA GmbH letterhead (with company logo, address, and legal footer)
- Sign and stamp (Firmenstempel)
- Scan as PDF and provide to candidate
- This letter substitutes for SCHUFA and Mietschuldenfreiheitsbescheinigung for new arrivals

**German version note:**
Landlords in Germany trust letters with a Firmenstempel (official company stamp). If your company does not use a physical stamp, a clearly formatted letterhead with all company registration details (HRB number, Amtsgericht) serves the same purpose.

**When to provide this letter:**
- When the employee has been in Germany less than 3 months (no SCHUFA yet)
- When the employee has no German payslips yet (first month of employment)
- When the employee is applying for a Jugendwohnheim or social housing program
- Always include alongside (not instead of) the employment contract

**Privacy note:**
Only share salary information with the candidate's explicit consent (GDPR/DSGVO). The letter may be shared with a specific landlord and should not be posted publicly.
